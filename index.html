Emergency Response

  • First-aid kit essentials: Include bandages, antiseptic, vet contact numbers, and a pet carrier.
  • Warning signs: Lethargy, vomiting, difficulty breathing, or bleeding needs immediate attention.
  • Stay calm: Your pet senses your emotions; keep them reassured during emergencies.
